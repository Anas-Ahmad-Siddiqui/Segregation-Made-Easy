Segregation Made Easy

This is a project by a group of students of VIT, Chennai. The aim of this project was to develop such a website that will be helpful in determining if a material queried by a user is "biodegradable", "dry" or "domestic hazardous".

The site may not be able to give result for every material but we are trying our best to make our dataset as huge as possible.
